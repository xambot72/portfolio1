ParaPod is a double-pronged strategy devised for the suburb of Mayfield, Newcastle, which aims to provide dwellings for 3,000 people, leveraging underutilized backyards and properties in the fight against housing unaffordability. Our solution is an arrangement of mass housing volumes along the Mayfield Urban Renewal Corridor, complemented by a modular system designed to attach and augment. These elements are to be rolled out in four stages.

ParaPod Mkii - Emergency Housing for a Changing Climate

Designed for rapid deployment in flood-prone Northern Australia, ParaPod mk 2 is a modular emergency housing system that prioritizes safety, dignity, and community resilience. Built with a lightweight yet durable scaffolding frame, its adaptable design accommodates individuals, families, and communal spaces. Thoughtfully crafted interiors use familiar materials to foster comfort and restore a sense of home. With a climate-sensitive approach, ParaPod mk 2 provides a practical and compassionate response to disaster relief.
